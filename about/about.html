About HA Solutions

Learn about our mission to transform organizations by delivering talent with the exact job skills needed.

Our Mission & Vision

HA Solutions was founded on the principle that talent exists everywhere, but opportunity does not. We bridge the gap between motivated individuals seeking technology careers and organizations needing skilled professionals.

Learn More

Our Mission

To create pathways to technology careers by providing free, comprehensive training and guaranteed job placement in Fortune 500 companies and government agencies.

Our Vision

A future where every motivated individual has access to the training and opportunities needed to succeed in technology, regardless of their background or prior experience.

The Problem We Solve

The technology skills gap leaves millions of job openings unfilled while talented individuals lack the specific skills employers demand.

Our Unique Approach

We eliminate risk for both candidates and employers through our hire-train-deploy model - training people in the exact skills needed before placement.

Impact & Results

Over 95% placement rate with industry-leading satisfaction scores from both candidates and employers.

Core Values

Excellence, integrity, diversity, and a commitment to transforming lives through technology careers.
